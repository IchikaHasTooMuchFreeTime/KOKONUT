About Kita Ikuyo

Kita Ikuyo is one of the main characters in the anime and manga series Bocchi the Rock! She serves as the guitarist and backing vocalist for the band Kessoku Band alongside Hitori "Bocchi" Gotoh, Nijika Ijichi, and Ryou Yamada.

Known for her cheerful and energetic personality, Kita brings life to the band with her vibrant stage presence and musical talent. Despite her sometimes airheaded nature, she's deeply passionate about music and her friends.

Fun Facts About Kita Ikuyo

Did you know these interesting tidbits about our favorite energetic guitarist?

🎸

Guitar Inspiration

Kita chose to play guitar after being inspired by a famous female guitarist she saw perform live in middle school.

2
🍓

Favorite Food

She has a massive sweet tooth and particularly loves strawberry shortcake, often eating it after performances.

3
🎤

Stage Fright

Despite her energetic stage presence, Kita actually gets nervous before every performance but channels it into energy.

4
📚

Academic Skills

She's surprisingly good at English but terrible at math, often asking Bocchi for help with homework.

5
🐶

Pet Lover

Kita adores animals and has a pet Shiba Inu named "Mochi" that she often posts about on social media.

6
🎧

Music Taste

Her favorite band outside of Kessoku Band is The Strokes, and she often listens to them for inspiration.
